# Stumble — Features (v1)

A Gen Z discovery app for cafes, restaurants, and bars in Indian metros. Sorted by **vibe** and **spend**, not star ratings.

## 1. Primary user
- 18–28 year olds in Indian metros who pick spots by aesthetic, feed-worthiness, and energy — and want to find them before they go mainstream.

## 2. The one thing they must be able to do
- **Open the app and instantly see nearby spots that match their vibe** (e.g. "indie + cheap" or "aesthetic + brunch").

## 3–5. Feature checklist

### Must-have (MVP — ship these first)
- [ ] **Discovery Feed** *(MVP)* — A scrollable card feed of nearby places, sorted by proximity and vibe match.
- [ ] **Vibe Filter** *(MVP)* — Multi-select tags (aesthetic, indie, study-friendly, late-night, brunch, pet-friendly) plus a spend tier (cheap / mid / splurge), remembered across sessions.
- [ ] **Place Detail Page** *(MVP)* — One screen per spot showing photos, vibe tags, spend label, and a save button.

### Must-have (V1, but after MVP)
- [ ] **Map View** — A toggle from the feed that shows pins on a map, colour-coded by vibe.
- [ ] **Save / Wishlist** — Bookmark places to a personal list that works offline.
- [ ] **Reviews + Photos** — Users post short text reviews and photos, tagged by vibe (no star ratings).

### Nice-to-have (can wait)
- [ ] **Trending Badge** — A small label on places gaining traction in the last 7 days.
- [ ] **Onboarding flow** — First-run screens for location permission, vibe preferences, and default spend tier.
- [ ] **Profile page** — Wishlist, saved vibe preferences, and the user's own submitted reviews in one place.
